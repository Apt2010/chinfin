3. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

1
Submit Request

Complete our online refund request form or contact our customer service team with your order details and reason for refund.

2

For physical products, you'll receive return instructions. Items must be in original condition with all packaging and certificates.

3
Inspection & Approval

Our quality team inspects returned items (if applicable) and approves refund if all conditions are met.

Refund Issued

Refund is processed to your original payment method. You'll receive confirmation via email.
